yogurt

Yogurt is a dairy product that is made by fermenting milk with the help of bacteria. Yogurt is a rich source of various essential nutrients, including calcium, protein, potassium, and vitamins B2 and B12. It is also a probiotic food, meaning it contains live bacteria that can provide health benefits to the gut.
